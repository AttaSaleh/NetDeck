Before using, I highly recommend downloading Hearthstone Deck Tracker (if you haven't already) to use in combination with this extension. It will make your life significantly easier, and lets you download and import decks directly into Hearthstone within seconds. If you are an Arena player, I also recommend signing up for HearthArena - it will coach your picks and ensure you get the best out of your draft. In combination, net.DEK, Hearthstone Deck Tracker, and HearthArena will completely change the way you play Hearthstone forever.

How to Use

When you come upon a net.DEK supported site, you will see a charm appear in your address bar when the site is loaded. Your address bar should look like the following:

Once the page is loaded, simply click the charm, you should see the following occur, and you will automatically download the decklist from that page to your computer.

That's it! It's that simple. The downloaded file is just a .txt file containing the full deck list shown on the website, and thanks to support with Hearthstone Deck Tracker, you can simply click Import -> From File and save the deck in your tracker! After that, Export -> To Hearthstone allows you to have the deck in game and ready to go in seconds!
